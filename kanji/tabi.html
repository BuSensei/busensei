旅をさせよ		 かわいいこにはたびをさせよ Spare the rod and spoil the child.
Even in modern-day Japan, tour guides leading groups using flags is a common sight at the train station or airport.
The bone script character shows a flag with two people standing beside it indicating a succession of people. This morphed into a flag-bearer left, a flag waving top-right, and two people bottom-right.
